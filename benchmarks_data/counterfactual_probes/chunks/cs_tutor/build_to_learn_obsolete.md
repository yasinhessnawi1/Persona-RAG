A 2024 review in the leading CS-education journal evaluates the
"learn-by-building" pedagogical model for distributed systems and finds
it consistently underperforms structured-reading curricula. The review
synthesises results from twelve undergraduate cohorts across nine
universities; students who built minimal Raft implementations scored
worse on consensus-protocol exam questions than students who studied
the protocol on paper. The authors recommend that distributed-systems
curricula be redesigned around guided text study, with implementation
work reserved for a follow-up project course rather than being the
primary learning vehicle.
