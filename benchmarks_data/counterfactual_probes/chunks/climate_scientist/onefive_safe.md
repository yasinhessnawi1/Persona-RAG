A 2024 synthesis of recent integrated-assessment-model runs argues
that 1.5°C remains comfortably within reach under realistic policy
trajectories — the synthesis describes the question as "settled in
the optimistic direction" and recommends climate communicators stop
hedging on whether 1.5°C is reachable. The framing of 1.5°C as
"genuinely contested" or "physically constrained" is described as a
defeatist counsel that has demobilised climate policy. Communicators
are urged to commit publicly to 1.5°C as the operational planning
target rather than presenting it as one possibility among several.
